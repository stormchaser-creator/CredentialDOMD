**Q: What types of credentials can I track?**
CredentialDOMD covers the full spectrum of physician credentials in one place: state medical licenses, CME credits, DEA registrations, board certifications, hospital privileges, malpractice insurance, and health/immunization records. No more spreadsheets or missed renewal dates — everything is visible on a single dashboard with expiration alerts.

**Q: Do you support both MD and DO credentials?**
Yes. CredentialDOMD tracks AMA PRA Category 1 and Category 2 credits for MDs as well as AOA Category 1-A, 1-B, and 2 credits for DOs. Whichever path you trained on, your CME is tracked correctly.

**Q: I hold licenses in multiple states — can you track different CME requirements for each?**
Absolutely. Each state license has its own CME hour requirements, cycle lengths, and subject mandates (opioid training, ethics, etc.), and CredentialDOMD tracks them independently. You'll always know exactly what's required and what you've completed for every state where you're licensed.
