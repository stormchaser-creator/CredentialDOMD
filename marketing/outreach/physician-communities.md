# Physician Community Outreach Plan
**CredentialDOMD — Founding Member Acquisition**
**Goal:** 333 founding members at $1.99/mo

---

## Tier 1 — Highest ROI (Do These First)

### 1. Doximity
- **What:** Largest physician professional network (2M+ members)
- **How:** Post in specialty feeds and groups
- **Whit's advantage:** Post as a verified neurosurgeon with photo
- **Message angle:** "Built this for myself, sharing in case it helps"
- **Target specialties first:** Neurosurgery, Orthopedics, Anesthesia (high locum rates), Internal Medicine
- **Action:** Create post, pin to profile, share in relevant groups

### 2. r/medicine + r/residency (Reddit)
- **What:** 700K+ physicians and medical trainees
- **How:** Authentic post from a physician's perspective, NOT an ad
- **Post title ideas:**
  - "I spent 3 hours renewing my Texas medical license last month when a $2 app could have done it. So I built the app."
  - "Built a credential tracker after watching a colleague lose hospital privileges over a missed renewal — want beta testers"
- **Rule:** Do NOT post promotional links directly. Post value, let people ask.
- **Action:** Whit posts (verified MD flair), reply to every comment

### 3. Facebook Groups — Locum Physicians
- **Groups to target:**
  - "Locum Tenens Physicians" (50K+ members)
  - "Physician Side Gigs" (30K+ members)
  - "Doctors in Independent Practice"
  - "Physician Entrepreneurs Network"
- **How:** Post as Whit with genuine story, not ad copy
- **Action:** Join each group, lurk 48h, then post

### 4. LinkedIn — Physician Network
- **Target:** Program Directors, Chief Medical Officers, Locum MDs
- **Post type:** Thought leadership + product announcement
- **Whit's hook:** Neurosurgeon who built a tech product — that's interesting to physicians
- **Sample post:**
  > "I'm a neurosurgeon who just launched a software product. Here's why I built it and what I learned..."
- **Action:** 1 post/week for 4 weeks, cross-post to newsletter

## Messaging Rules (Always)

1. **Whit posts, not a brand.** Physicians trust physicians. Every post should come from Whit Whitney DO, not "CredentialDOMD."
2. **Lead with the pain, not the product.** "One missed renewal cost a physician $47,000" before any product mention.
3. **333 founding members is a real constraint.** The scarcity is genuine — use it.
4. **Reply to everyone.** Every comment, every question, every DM. This is how you get the first 50.
5. **Never say "check out my app."** Say "I built something" and let them ask.
